Some Great Authors



Yashavant Kanetkar

Yashavant P. Kanetkar is an Indian computer science author, known for his books on programming languages. He has authored several books on topics such as C, C++, VC++, C#, .NET, DirectX and COM programming.

Robert Lafore

Robert W. Lafore is a computer programmer and systems analyst and entrepreneur. He coined the term "interactive fiction", and was an early software developer in this field. He is a best-selling writer in the field of computer programming.

David A. Patterson

He is an American computer pioneer and academic who has held the position of Professor of Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley since 1976.Patterson is noted for his pioneering contributions to RISC processor design, having coined the term RISC, and by leading the Berkeley RISC project.He is also noted for his research on RAID disks..
